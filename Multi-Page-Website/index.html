My Speciality
Currently, my friends and I are working on a project to build an rc-plane out of styrafoam.
We are far ahead of schedule, and estimate that we will be done with the assembly of the plane two weeks from now.
We have spend a long time waiting for our parts to arrive, however, we have made the entire body of the plane, and will assemble the rest as the parts arrive.
Every day, my friends and I go to the makerspace to work on the plane, and to share ideas on how to solve the problem we run into.
