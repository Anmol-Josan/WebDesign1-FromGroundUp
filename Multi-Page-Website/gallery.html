This page shows the parts for our plane

ESC
This is the ESC(essential speed controller) for the rc plane. The ESC controlls the speed for the motors.
Motor
This is the 2700kv motor for the rc plane. The motor is what makes the plane take off by spinning in circles
Battery
This is the huge battery for the rc plane. It can power our plane for several hours
Transmitter
This is the transmitter for the rc plane. It tells our esc how much power to give to the motors.
All together
This is all these pieces together for the rc plane. They work together to make the plane fly.

Starting next week, we will start to work more on the plane, and put all of the electronics inside of it. After that, we will make the wings using the CNC, and then the plane should work. The components that we used are some of the best we could find, so we are fearful of breaking it.
