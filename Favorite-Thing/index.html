Electronics:

In my free time, I often like building electronics, and making simple and complex circuts with them. Currently, my friends and I are building a 4.5 foot rc plane after school for fun. As of my last test, which was in 8th grade, I am currently level 5 in all of the tools in the maker-space such as the vinyl cutter, the laser-cutter, and the 3d-printer. I am also experienced in using a soldering iron, and in fact, in the rc plane that I mentioned earlier, my job will be to assemble most of the electronics and the plane body. We estimate that somewhere in the middle of the winter-trimester, we will be finished with making our plane, but that is a very rough estimate, because we don't know when we will have time to work on it, so most likely, we will finish before winter-break. Thankfully, the maker-space is making our efforts much easier, as we do not have to worry about gathering materials, or on the technology required to shape said materials.
